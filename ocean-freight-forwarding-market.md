<p><p>Ocean freight forwarding involves various market types based on cargo volume and shipping requirements. FCL (Full Container Load) is used when a single shipper occupies an entire container, ideal for large shipments. LCL (Less than Container Load) combines cargo from multiple shippers, optimizing costs for smaller shipments. Other services may include breakbulk shipping for irregularly shaped cargo and roll-on/roll-off (RoRo) for vehicles. Each type serves different logistical needs, offering flexibility and efficiency in global shipping.</p></p>
