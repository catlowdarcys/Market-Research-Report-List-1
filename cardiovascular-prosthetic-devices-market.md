<p><p>The cardiovascular prosthetic devices market is divided into two main types: cardiac prosthetic devices and vascular prosthetic devices. Cardiac prosthetic devices include artificial heart valves, pacemakers, and left ventricular assist devices, aimed at improving heart function and treatment of cardiac conditions. Vascular prosthetic devices, such as stents, grafts, and balloon catheters, focus on restoring and maintaining blood flow through arteries and veins. Together, these devices play a crucial role in managing cardiovascular diseases and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
