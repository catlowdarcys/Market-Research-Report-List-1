<p><ul><li>Layer 2</li><li>Layer 3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MPLS IP VPN services are divided into Layer 2 and Layer 3 markets. In Layer 2 services, the focus is on providing data link connectivity, allowing customers to manage their IP routing while benefiting from a secure, private network. Layer 3 services, on the other hand, include routing capabilities, where the service provider manages not only the data transport but also the IP addressing and routing, delivering a fully managed solution for comprehensive network connectivity.</p></p>
