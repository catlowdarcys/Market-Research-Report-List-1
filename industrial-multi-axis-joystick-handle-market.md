<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Joystick Handle</li><li>Potentiometer Joystick Handle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Multi Axis Joystick Handle market is primarily segmented into two types: Hall Effect joystick handles and potentiometer joystick handles. Hall Effect joystick handles utilize magnetic fields to detect position, offering greater durability and resistance to wear, making them suitable for demanding environments. In contrast, potentiometer joystick handles operate using variable resistors to measure movement, often providing a more cost-effective solution but with less durability. Both types serve various industrial applications, enhancing precision in control systems.</p></p>
