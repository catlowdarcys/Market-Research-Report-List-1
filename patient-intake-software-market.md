<p><p>Patient intake software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on healthcare facilities' servers, offering control over data and systems but requiring significant upfront costs and maintenance efforts. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, easier updates, and accessibility from any device with internet access. This model reduces IT burdens and costs, making it increasingly popular among healthcare providers looking for flexibility and efficiency in managing patient information.</p></p>
