<p><p>The Automotive Camera Module Adhesives market is categorized into two main types: UV Curable and Non-UV Curable. UV Curable adhesives utilize ultraviolet light for rapid curing, making them ideal for high-speed manufacturing processes and providing excellent optical clarity for camera lenses. In contrast, Non-UV Curable adhesives cure through heat or moisture, offering versatility in various environmental conditions. Both types are essential for ensuring robust bonding and protection of camera modules in automotive applications, contributing to safety and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
