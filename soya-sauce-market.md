<p><strong>The Soya Sauce Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brewed</li><li>Blended</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy sauce market is primarily categorized into brewed and blended types. Brewed soy sauce is made through traditional fermentation, resulting in a rich flavor profile and complex aromas. It is often favored by purists and culinary enthusiasts. On the other hand, blended soy sauce is produced by mixing brewed soy sauce with hydrolyzed vegetable protein, offering a quicker and more cost-effective alternative. This type tends to have a milder taste and is commonly used in mass-produced products.</p></p>
