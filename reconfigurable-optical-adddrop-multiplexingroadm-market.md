<p><p>Reconfigurable Optical Add/Drop Multiplexing (ROADM) enables dynamic routing of optical signals in fiber networks. The market comprises several key components: Wavelength Selective Switches (WSS), which allow specific wavelengths to be added or dropped without disrupting others; Optical Channel Monitoring (OCM) for real-time signal quality assessment and performance analysis; and Variable Optical Attenuators (VOAs), which adjust signal strength to optimize performance. Together, these technologies enhance network flexibility, efficiency, and reliability in optical communication systems.</p></p>
