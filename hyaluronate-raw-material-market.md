<p><p>The Hyaluronate Raw Material Market comprises two primary types: microbial fermentation and animal tissue. Microbial fermentation involves producing hyaluronic acid through the fermentation of specific bacteria, offering a consistent and purer product with reduced risk of contamination. In contrast, the animal tissue method derives hyaluronic acid from animal sources, such as rooster combs or connective tissues, which can lead to variability in quality and potential allergic reactions. Both methods serve various applications in the pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and food industries.</p></p>
