<p><p>Analog ammeters are electromechanical devices used to measure electric current in a circuit. They display readings through a moving needle on a calibrated dial, providing visual indication of current levels. These instruments are known for their simplicity, reliability, and immediate response to current changes, making them suitable for various applications, including educational settings, laboratories, and industrial processes.</p><p>The Analog Ammeters Market is poised for significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 11% during the forecast period.
