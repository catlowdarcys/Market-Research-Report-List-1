<p><p>The automotive carpet market primarily consists of two types: needled carpet and tufted carpet. Needled carpets are made by interlocking fibers through a process that creates a dense, durable surface, ideal for heavy-duty applications in vehicles. Tufted carpets, on the other hand, feature yarns stitched into a backing material, offering versatility and design options. Both types cater to various vehicle models and user preferences, balancing aesthetics, comfort, and performance while contributing to overall vehicle insulation and noise reduction.</p></p>
