<p><ul><li>Resonators</li><li>Delay Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensor market comprises two main types: resonators and delay lines. Resonators utilize SAW technology to detect changes in frequency, often used in sensing applications like pressure and temperature. Delay lines, on the other hand, measure time delays of waves traveling across the surface, useful for applications requiring precise measurement of parameters such as displacement or chemical concentrations. Both types leverage SAW principles for high sensitivity and reliability in various industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
