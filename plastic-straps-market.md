<p><p>The plastic straps market primarily consists of polypropylene (PP) and polyethylene terephthalate (PET) straps, each serving distinct applications. PP straps are lightweight, cost-effective, and suitable for light to medium-duty packaging, often used in bundling and securing products. In contrast, PET straps offer superior strength, resistance to stretching, and durability for heavy-duty applications, making them ideal for securing heavy loads in transportation and storage. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring secure packaging and enhanced product safety.</p></p>
