<p><strong>The Inkjet Marking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIJ</li><li>DOD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet marking system market primarily includes Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop-On-Demand (DOD) technologies. CIJ systems continuously circulate ink and jet it onto surfaces, making them ideal for high-speed production lines, particularly in packaging and coding applications. In contrast, DOD systems eject ink droplets only when needed, offering flexibility for various substrates and lower ink consumption, often used in industrial printing and high-resolution applications. Both technologies cater to diverse marking needs across multiple industries.</p></p>
