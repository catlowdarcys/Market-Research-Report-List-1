<p><p>The food clarifiers market comprises two main types: solid separation and solid-liquid separation. Solid-liquid separation involves separating solids from liquids within a mixture, commonly used for clarifying broths, oils, or beverages. Both methods enhance product quality, improve shelf life, and meet regulatory standards while catering to various food processing industries.</p></p>
