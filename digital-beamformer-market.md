<p><ul><li>Adaptive Beamformer</li><li>Fixed Beamformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital beamformer market consists of two primary types: adaptive and fixed beamformers. Adaptive beamformers use algorithms to adjust the direction and shape of the antenna radiation pattern in real-time, enhancing signal quality and reducing interference. This flexibility makes them ideal for dynamic environments. In contrast, fixed beamformers have predetermined radiation patterns, offering simplicity and reliability for stationary applications. Both types cater to various industries, such as telecommunications, radar, and satellite communications, enhancing performance and efficiency in signal processing.</p></p>
