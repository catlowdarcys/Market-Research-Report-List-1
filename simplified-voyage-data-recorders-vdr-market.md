<p><p>The Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (S-VDR) is a device designed to capture and store essential navigational and communications data from a vessel, enhancing maritime safety. There are two main market types: Float Free Capsule S-VDR and Fixed Capsule S-VDR. Float Free Capsule S-VDR is designed to automatically release and float free in emergencies, ensuring data retrieval, while Fixed Capsule S-VDR is securely mounted on the vessel, providing continuous data recording without the ability to float free, suitable for environments with lower risks.</p></p>
