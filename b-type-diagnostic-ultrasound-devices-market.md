<p><strong>The B type Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>B-type diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into three main scanning types: fanshaped scanning, linear scanning, and mixed type scanning. Fanshaped scanning utilizes a curved array to produce a wide sector image, ideal for cardiac and abdominal assessments. Linear scanning employs a straight array, providing high-resolution images, particularly for superficial structures like veins and arteries. Mixed type scanning combines features of both methods, offering flexibility in imaging across various applications, thus catering to diverse diagnostic needs in medical ultrasound.</p></p>
