<p><p>The interventional radiology market encompasses various imaging systems essential for minimally invasive procedures. MRI systems provide detailed images of soft tissues, crucial for diagnosing conditions. Ultrasound imaging systems utilize sound waves for real-time visuals, aiding in precise interventions. CT scanners deliver cross-sectional images, allowing for accurate assessments of complex structures. Angiography systems are specialized for visualizing blood vessels, enabling doctors to perform targeted treatments for vascular issues. Together, these technologies enhance procedural accuracy and patient outcomes in interventional radiology.</p></p>
