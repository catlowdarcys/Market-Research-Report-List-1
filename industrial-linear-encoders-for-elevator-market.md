<p><strong>The Industrial Linear Encoders for Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Linear Encoders</li><li>Absolute Linear Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial linear encoders for the elevator market come in two primary types: incremental and absolute linear encoders. Incremental linear encoders measure displacement by providing a series of pulses as the elevator moves, allowing for relative position tracking. In contrast, absolute linear encoders deliver a unique position value for each point on their range, ensuring that the elevator knows its exact position at all times. Both types enhance elevator performance, accuracy, and reliability in industrial applications.</p></p>
