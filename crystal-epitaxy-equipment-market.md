<p><p>Crystal epitaxy equipment encompasses various techniques for producing high-quality crystalline layers on substrates. Vapor Phase Epitaxy (VPE) utilizes gaseous precursors to deposit materials, ideal for compound semiconductors. Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD) employs organometallic compounds, widely used in creating light-emitting diodes and solar cells. Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) facilitates precise atomic layer growth under ultra-high vacuum conditions, crucial for advanced semiconductor devices. Each method serves distinct applications in electronics, optoelectronics, and nanotechnology, driving innovation in the industry.</p></p>
