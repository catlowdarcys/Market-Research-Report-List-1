<p><p>Clinical Laboratory Automation Systems encompass three primary market types: Pre-Analysis Systems, Under Analysis Systems, and Post-Analysis Systems. Pre-Analysis Systems streamline specimen handling and preparation, enhancing sample integrity and reducing turnaround times. Under Analysis Systems pertain to automated analytical processes that perform tests with precision and efficiency, minimizing human error. Post-Analysis Systems focus on data management, reporting, and interpretation, facilitating informed decision-making. Together, these systems improve operational efficiency, accuracy, and productivity in clinical laboratories.</p></p>
