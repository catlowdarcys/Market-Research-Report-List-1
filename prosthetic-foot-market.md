<p><ul><li>Mechanical Foot</li><li>Microprocessor Foot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prosthetic foot market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical feet and microprocessor feet. Mechanical feet are passive devices that rely on springs and hinges to provide basic functionality, offering durability and low maintenance. In contrast, microprocessor feet incorporate advanced technology, featuring sensors and computerized components that adapt to varying terrains and user movements. This results in enhanced stability and a more natural gait, making them suitable for users seeking higher performance and comfort in their mobility solutions.</p></p>
