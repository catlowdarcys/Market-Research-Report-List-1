<p><ul><li>Low Level</li><li>High Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical palletizers are categorized primarily into low-level and high-level systems. Low-level palletizers operate at a height similar to the conveyor system, allowing products to be placed directly onto pallets at a lower level, which enhances ease of access and minimizes ergonomic risks. High-level palletizers, on the other hand, work at elevated heights, stacking products onto pallets from above, enabling high-density stacking and efficient space utilization. Both types cater to varying production needs and space constraints in warehouses and manufacturing environments.</p></p>
