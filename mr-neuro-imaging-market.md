<p><strong>The MR Neuro Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting</li><li>Permanent Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MR Neuro Imaging market is primarily segmented into two types: superconducting and permanent magnet systems. Superconducting MRI machines utilize superconducting materials to generate powerful magnetic fields, enabling high-resolution imaging suitable for detailed neurological assessments. In contrast, permanent magnet MRI systems rely on permanent magnets to create a magnetic field, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution, although with lower imaging resolution. Both types serve distinct roles in neuroimaging, catering to various clinical needs and operational environments.</p></p>
