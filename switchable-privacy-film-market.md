<p><p>The switchable privacy film market comprises two main types: non-adhesive film and self-adhesive film. Non-adhesive film can be easily installed on surfaces without the need for adhesive, making it suitable for temporary applications and easy removal. In contrast, self-adhesive film features an integrated adhesive layer, allowing for a more permanent installation on various substrates. Both types offer privacy on demand through technologies like liquid crystal or polymer dispersion, catering to diverse needs in residential, commercial, and automotive settings.</p></p>
