<p><p>Vacuum drum dryers are specialized equipment used for drying heat-sensitive materials in various industries. The market features two main types: single drum and double drum dryers. Single drum dryers consist of one rotating drum, ideal for smaller batches and simpler processes. In contrast, double drum dryers use two drums, providing increased surface area for efficient drying of larger quantities. Both types operate under vacuum conditions, minimizing thermal degradation and ensuring quality in the final product while optimizing energy consumption.</p></p>
