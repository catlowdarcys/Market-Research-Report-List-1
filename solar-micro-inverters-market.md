<p><p>The Solar Micro Inverter market is divided into two main types: Grid-Connected and Grid-Off Micro Inverters. Grid-Connected Micro Inverters operate in conjunction with the electrical grid, allowing solar energy to be fed back into the grid while optimizing energy production from individual solar panels. In contrast, Grid-Off Micro Inverters function independently, powering off-grid systems and storing excess energy for later use, making them suitable for remote locations or independent solar setups. Both types enhance energy efficiency and system performance.</p></p>
