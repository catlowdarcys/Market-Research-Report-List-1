<p><p>The personal flotation devices (PFD) market is categorized into three main types: foam life jackets, inflatable life jackets, and hybrid life jackets. Inflatable life jackets are lightweight and compact, deploying when activated, offering comfort for extended wear. Each type meets varying user needs and preferences in aquatic environments.</p></p>
