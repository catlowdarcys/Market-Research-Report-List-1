<p><p>The display material market encompasses various components essential for LCDs. Polarizers enhance image clarity by filtering light. Substrates, typically glass or plastic, provide structural support. Color filter layers create vibrant images by allowing specific colors to pass through. Liquid crystals manipulate light to form images when electrically activated. The Backlighting Unit (BLU) illuminates the display, ensuring visibility in various conditions. Other materials may include adhesives and coatings, contributing to overall performance and durability of LCD technology.</p></p>
