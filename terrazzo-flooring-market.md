<p><p>Terrazzo flooring is available in two main types: Inorganic Terrazzo and Epoxy Terrazzo. Inorganic Terrazzo is composed of cement and natural aggregates, offering durability and a classic aesthetic suitable for high-traffic areas. It's known for its strength and resistance to wear. Epoxy Terrazzo, on the other hand, uses epoxy resin as a binder, allowing for more vibrant colors and complex designs. This type is ideal for environments where flexibility and quick installation are essential, providing a seamless and attractive finish.</p></p>
