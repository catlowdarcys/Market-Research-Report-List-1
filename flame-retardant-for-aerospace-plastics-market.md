<p><ul><li>Additive</li><li>Reactive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flame Retardant for Aerospace Plastics Market is categorized into two main types: additive and reactive. Additive flame retardants are externally applied to plastics, providing a protective layer that helps prevent ignition and slow the spread of flames. In contrast, reactive flame retardants are incorporated into the polymer matrix during manufacturing, forming a chemical bond that enhances the material's inherent fire-resistant properties. Both types are critical in meeting stringent safety regulations and ensuring the reliability of aerospace applications.</p></p>
