<p><p>The soil remediation market encompasses various techniques to treat contaminated soil. Bioremediation utilizes microorganisms to break down pollutants naturally. Chemical oxidation involves applying chemicals to transform contaminants into less harmful substances. Stabilization/solidification methods encapsulate pollutants in a solid matrix, reducing their mobility. Soil washing employs water and additives to separate contaminants from soil particles. Thermal desorption heats soil to evaporate volatile contaminants, facilitating clean-up. Each method offers unique advantages depending on contamination type and site conditions.</p></p>
