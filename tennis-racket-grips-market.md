<p><p>The tennis racket grips market is divided into two main types: overgrips and replacement grips. Tennis racket overgrips are thin layers applied over the existing grip to enhance comfort, absorb moisture, and provide better control. They are easily replaceable and allow players to customize their feel. On the other hand, tennis racket replacement grips are thicker, serving to replace the original grip entirely when it wears out or needs a different texture. Both types are essential for optimizing performance and player comfort.</p></p>
