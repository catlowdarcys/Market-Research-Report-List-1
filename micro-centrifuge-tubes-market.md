<p><p>The micro centrifuge tubes market features three primary types based on their base design: conical base, round bottom base, and flat bottom base. Conical base tubes are designed for optimal sedimentation of samples, making them ideal for a variety of biological applications. Round bottom base tubes facilitate easy mixing and are suitable for working with viscous solutions. Flat bottom base tubes provide stable support for certain assays and are fit for applications requiring surface contact, each serving unique laboratory needs.</p></p>
